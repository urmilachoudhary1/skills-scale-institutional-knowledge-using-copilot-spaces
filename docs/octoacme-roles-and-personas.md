## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts elicit requirements, clarify business rules, and ensure functionality maps to business goals. They bridge the gap between stakeholders and the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Work with Product Managers to gather and document requirements/user stories
- Maintain traceability of requirements to acceptance criteria and deliverables
- Facilitate clarification of business logic and edge cases with developers and QA
- Support change management and scope adjustments

### Goals
- Reduce misunderstandings or rework caused by unclear requirements
- Aid prioritization through business impact analysis

### Typical Communication
- Backlog grooming and requirements workshops with PdM, Developers, and QA
- Written user stories, acceptance criteria, and clarifications in docs or tickets

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers champion usability and customer experience, turning requirements into wireframes/prototypes and validating usability through research.

### Responsibilities
- Conceptualize and prototype new features based on requirements
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Provide design assets and guidelines to Developers/QA

### Goals
- Improve feature adoption and customer satisfaction
- Reduce rework due to unclear or unvalidated designs

### Typical Communication
- Share prototypes and gather feedback from Product Manager, Developers, and end-users
- Document design decisions and rationale

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers automate deployment, facilitate CI/CD, and support high availability and observability of released features.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain automated build, test, and deployment pipelines
- Implement infrastructure as code and monitoring solutions
- Respond to incidents and support rapid recovery

### Goals
- Accelerate safe, repeatable releases
- Minimize downtime and infrastructure friction for the team

### Typical Communication
- Work closely with Developers/QA to integrate testing and deployment
- Share metrics and alerts with Project Manager and stakeholders

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads advocate for user needs post-launch, gather feedback, and ensure documentation and support readiness for new releases.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and triage user feedback/issues
- Surface trends and pain points to Product Manager
- Collaborate with Developers/QA on bug fixes and edge cases
- Maintain user guides and support documentation

### Goals
- Shorten feedback loop from users to roadmap
- Enhance customer satisfaction and support readiness

### Typical Communication
- Regular feedback summaries to Product Manager and Project Manager
- Participation in incident reviews to represent the customer voice
